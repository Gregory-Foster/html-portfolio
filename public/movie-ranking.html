The Best Movies According To Gregory

My top 5 movies of all-times

Selma Lord Selma.

In 1965, during the turbulent early days of the right to vote movement.

The Bodyguard

Whitney Houston, movie superstar.
Fans wanter to hear her, touch her,
but one wants to kill her.

The Great Debaters

Based on a true story, Danzel Washington
leads a group of underdog African American college
students into a historical powerhouse

The Seventh Sign

Time is running out and revelation is at hand.

The Autobiography of Miss Jane Pitman

Cicely Tyson plays Miss Pitman a 110-year-old slave
sharing the memories of black experiences from the civil war
to the end of the civil rights movement.
